Emergency Therapy Appointments

We offer Emergency Therapy by phone with one of our qualified therapists who are based in London Marylebone and Chelsea, Worthing in West Sussex, Brighton or Seaford in East Sussex.

You can discuss any issues you have. Perhaps you:

  • are feeling anxious and it will be a while before you will see anyone due to long waiting lists via the NHS
  • have outstanding questions after a visit to the doctor’s, or
  • simply just need some friendly and caring support or advice right now.
image of girl

We don't want you to suffer so we offer an alternative.

At Morton Harbour, you will receive person to person support where we guide you to the next step and provide immediate help.

Your Emergency Therapy appointment will be with one of our professional Hypnotherapists, Counsellors or Psychotherapists. They will be on the end of a phone wherever you are based.

The cost of this service is £25 and an appointment lasts for 20 minutes, payment is made via Paypal, book a call back now using the form below.
