About Us

Our therapists are fully qualified and experienced and offer Psychotherapy, Hypnotherapy and Counselling. We offer a free inital consultation of 30 mins and can see you in one of our clinics in London, Worthing, Brighton or Seaford.

Fully qualified & experienced
Magda and Christian both graduated from the esteemed Essex Institute (EICH Brighton Ltd) and are members of the Association for Professional Hypnosis and Psychotherapy and The National Register of Psychotherapists and Counsellors. Together they have over 18 years of experience as qualified therapists. Magda is also registered with the Complementary and Natural Healthcare Council.
Emergency appointments
We offer Emergency Therapy by phone. Perhaps you are feeling anxious and it will be a while before you will see anyone due to long waiting lists via the NHS, you have outstanding questions after a visit to the doctor’s, or simply just need some friendly and caring support or advice right now.
Various clinic locations
We offer therapy at any one of our multiple locations in London, Worthing, Brighton and Seaford. We can also offer therapy via Skype, FaceTime or phone where suitable. Contact us for more information or to arrange a free, no obligation, intial 30 mins consultation.
Trustworthy & confidential
As members of the APHP, we are bound by their Code of Ethics. It is only possible to join the Association via an invitation or through training with an accredited hypnotherapy school, meaning that each member is known to have received thorough training in hypnosis and hypnotherapy and is competent and conscientious.

As with all registered professional therapists we are fully covered by professional indemnity insurance.

Photo of Magda

Magda Harbour

Co-Director MAPHP MNRPC

Magda is a very experienced hypnotherapist, psychotherapist and counsellor, having completed more than 14,000 therapy hours. She has successfully treated hundreds of clients since graduating from the eminent Essex Institute in 2009.

She specialises in using talking therapies for the treatment of anxiety, depression, obsessive-compulsive disorders, disordered eating, confidence and self-esteem issues. Magda works in all three of our clinics in Central London, Worthing, West Sussex, Brighton and Seaford, East Sussex.

She prides herself on offering a holistic service which fully supports her clients and does so in a caring professional manner. This allows her to make her clients feel very safe and comfortable.

Before working as a therapist, Magda was employed as a management consultant in the City of London, giving her a keen analytical mind and an understanding of high pressured lifestyles. She enjoys walking, studying, music, a little retail therapy and spending time with her family.

“I take the well-being of every client extremely personally. My passion is to help clients make the positive changes they need, so they can feel good and confident in themselves.
At Morton Harbour, Talking Therapies are accessible to everyone. My personal aspiration is to finally eliminate the taboo of talking about mental health, that leaves so many of us suffering in silence.”
