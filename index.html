Who is affected by mental health issues?

1 in 4 people are adversely affected by mental health issues each year and we want every individual to reach and enjoy their full potential … especially the growing number of our young people. Psychotherapy, Hypnotherapy and Counselling help everyone. Read what some of Find out what our clients sayour clients say.

Long NHS waiting lists

Have you walked into a Doctor’s surgery feeling a little down or feeling anxious? Many people have and they leave with some pills and a diagnosis of a mental health illness, then there is nowhere to turn as there are long waiting lists for CBT or counselling on the NHS.

We can offer immediate support

We think long waiting times for support is unacceptable and we offer an alternative. At Morton Harbour you will receive person to person support where we guide you to the next step and provide immediate help. Find out about our emergency appointmentsemergency service appointments.

Ending the stigma

We aim to create a world where mental illness is viewed as an acceptable and normal by-product of our modern lives. So we want to finally dispel the taboo that still surrounds mental health and be at the forefront of providing personal and accessible therapy that provides openness and life progression … no more suffering in silence. We can help you with many mental illnesses, including Depression, Anxiety, Panic Attacks, Life Changes, Fears and Phobias, Weight Issues, Eating Disorders, Sexual Problems and other related issues. You can tackle any of these with our therapists in counselling, hypnotherapy and psychotherapy in Central London, Worthing in West Sussex and Seaford, East Sussex.

Meet our therapists

Our therapists are fully qualified and experienced and offer Psychotherapy, Hypnotherapy and Counselling. We offer a free inital consultation of 30 mins and can see you in one of our clinics in London, Worthing, Brighton or Seaford.
