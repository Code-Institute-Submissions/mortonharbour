Psychotherapy and Hypnotherapy Therapists | Central London, Worthing, Brighton and Seaford

We can help you with therapies that will overcome your mental health issues, as well as emergency appointments.

Our clinics provide Psychotherapy, Hypnotherapy and Counselling in Central London (Marylebone), Worthing in West Sussex and Seaford in East Sussex. By listening and understanding, we are able to equip you with the tools you need to rediscover your own mental strength, helping you to overcome your problems and release you to enjoy living your life to the full again. Many people have and they leave with some pills and a diagnosis of a mental health illness, then there is nowhere to turn as there are long waiting lists for CBT or counselling on the NHS. We aim to create a world where mental illness is viewed as an acceptable and normal by-product of our modern lives. So we want to finally dispel the taboo that still surrounds mental health and be at the forefront of providing personal and accessible therapy that provides openness and life progression … no more suffering in silence. We can help you with a many mental illnesses, including Depression, Anxiety, Panic Attacks, Life Changes, Fears and Phobias, Weight Issues, Eating Disorders, Sexual Problems and other related issues. You can tackle any of these with our therapists in counselling, hypnotherapy and psychotherapy in Central London, Worthing in West Sussex and Seaford, East Sussex.
